## Asset Policy

The skill intentionally does not mirror the full text, images, videos, or editorial layouts from Google Design or Material Design. Use those sites as source references and inspiration.

Bundled local assets are limited to official Material Symbols variable fonts, a codepoint index, CSS helpers, and starter token scaffolds. The Material Symbols assets are provided under Apache License 2.0 by Google in the official `google/material-design-icons` repository.

If a user asks to use Google Design article imagery or Google product screenshots directly, treat that as a separate rights/permission question. Prefer recreating the design approach with original assets.
